### [boot #8] Chinese pantheon for the Qing state religion (confucianism).
### Vanilla Imperator ships the confucianism religion with NO deities, so the Pantheon panel
### stood empty for the Qing. This populates it with twelve venerated figures drawn from the
### four great traditions of the realm — Confucian sages (儒), Taoist immortals (道), Buddhist
### figures (佛), and folk/heaven deities (民間/天) — so the vanilla Pantheon & Holy Sites panel
### is meaningful for a Qing player.
###
### Every mechanical body (icon = / passive_modifier svalue / omen svalue / on_activate effect /
### deity_category) is cloned verbatim from the PROVEN base-game deity structure in
### common/deities/00_generic.txt (which loads in this mod — its svalues, icons and apotheosis
### effects are base-game vanilla, inherited at runtime). Only the deity KEY, the bound religion
### (region1 -> confucianism), and the display loc are changed.
### Slot balance mirrors 00_generic: 3 war / 3 economy / 3 culture / 3 fertility.
### No BOM (deities/ convention). Names localized in localization/english/qing_deities_l_english.yml.
###
### HOLY SITES are NOT set here — the engine reads holy_site=<this deity key> in PROVINCE history
### (setup/provinces/*.txt), matching the proven Invictus/base-game convention (holy_site is a
### province field, never a deity field). The province assignments are noted in a comment beside
### each deity and applied in the setup files.
