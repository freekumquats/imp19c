### Vanilla Imperator ships confucianism with no deities of its own, so the Pantheon panel fell
### through to the 8 generic region1 deities (deity_war1 ...) — functional, but shown as raw
### unlocalized keys. This gives confucianism EIGHT proper Chinese deities.
###
### CRITICAL — STRUCTURE MUST MIRROR THE WORKING GENERIC SET (00_generic.txt): exactly EIGHT
### deities, TWO per category (war / economy / culture / fertility), each body cloned VERBATIM from
### the matching generic deity (icon / passive_modifier svalue / omen svalue / on_activate effect /
### deity_category). Only the KEY, the bound religion (region1 -> confucianism), and the display loc
### change. The PRIOR attempt shipped TWELVE deities (3 per category) + holy sites placed in

### structure ("list of none", malformed layout, empty Holy Sites). This file deliberately does NOT
