### [#63 2026-07-23] IDEOLOGY THINKER-PANTHEONS (Design 3 — Religion → Ideology).
### Each of the 6 ideology religions (01_ideologies.txt) gets EIGHT thinker-deities, TWO per
### category (war/economy/culture/fertility), mirroring the working Confucian reskin
### (03_confucian_pantheon.txt) which itself mirrors the generic 8/2-2-2-2 set (00_generic.txt).
### CRITICAL: every body below is a VERBATIM clone of the matching generic deity
### (icon / passive_modifier svalue / omen svalue / on_activate / deity_category) — only the KEY,
### the bound religion, and the display loc change. The generic set is SUPPRESSED for these six
### religions in 00_generic.txt (NOR over confucianism + the 6 ideologies) so each ideology shows THESE eight, not the
### raw generic keys or a doubled 16-deity list (the panel-break class). NO holy sites (empty is the
### proven baseline). DB registration: setup/main/deities/03_ideologies.txt (keys 1500-1547, no BOM).
### Themes: war=militancy, economy=political-economy, culture=culture/press, fertility=social-base.
### Loc in localization/english/qing_ideology_deities_l_english.yml. UTF-8 BOM (CJK in comments).
