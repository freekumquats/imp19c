# Research: African polities circa 1763 (BT-J) — reference only, no map edits yet
Source: background research agent 2026-07-19, Wikipedia-synthesized (see UNESCO Gen. Hist. Africa for depth).

## HIGHEST-PRIORITY centralized states (strong tags) to fill gaps
- West Africa: Ashanti (Ghana interior, Kumasi), Oyo (SW Nigeria/Yorubaland, peak), Dahomey
  (Benin, Tegbesu, Oyo tributary), Bamana/Ségou (Mali, Niger valley), Kanem-Bornu (Chad/NE Nigeria, declining),
  Benin Empire (S Nigeria/Edo), Hausa city-states (N Nigeria: Kano/Katsina/Zaria/Gobir/Daura — pre-Sokoto),
  Futa Jallon (Guinea, Islamic federation), Kong (Ivory Coast/Burkina, fragmented post-1745).
- Central Africa: Lunda (S DRC/N Angola/NW Zambia, ~300k km² confederation), Luba (SE DRC),
  Kazembe/E.Lunda (N Zambia), Kongo (fragmented), Kuba (S-central DRC), Loango (ROC/Gabon coast).
- East Africa: Ethiopian Empire (Gondarine, cusp of Zemene Mesafint), Buganda (C Uganda, expanding),
  Bunyoro (W Uganda, near peak), Nkore/Ankole, Rwanda, Burundi (decentralized), Swahili coast under Oman
  (Kilwa/Mombasa/Pate/Zanzibar).
- Southern Africa: Rozvi (Zimbabwe plateau), Mutapa (fragmented ~1760), Dutch Cape Colony/VOC (SW Cape,
  trekboers inland), Khoikhoi + San (stateless), pre-Mfecane Nguni (Xhosa/Zulu/Swazi) + Sotho-Tswana chiefdoms.
- NE/Sahel/Sudan: Funj/Sennar (Hamaj Regency 1762+, puppet sultan), Darfur (Keira dynasty centralizing),
  Wadai (E Chad), Bagirmi (S-C Chad, indep from Bornu 1751+).
- North Africa: Morocco (Alaouite, Sharifian monarchy), Ottoman regencies Algiers/Tunis(Husainid)/
  Tripoli(Karamanli), Ottoman Egypt (Mamluk-dominated de facto; Ali Bey al-Kabir Sheikh al-Balad 1763).
- Madagascar: Sakalava (W coast: Menabe + Boina, firearms/slave trade), Imerina (central, fragmented pre-1787).
